SunBorn, Independent Games Festival
November 2006

A multiplayer arcade action title developed in under a year for the Independent Games Festival Student Competition. The Graphics boast celshading, geomorphing terrain, day/night cycles, and cloud imposters, and the animation was doing using Vicon Motion Capture. The unfinished client is free and available to play.

Experimental Gameplay Project
Spring 2006

Games are developed in under a week to explore the gameplay mechanics of a particular theme. The link leads you to my first title, Homework Junkie, which was developed under the theme of 'scale'. The second title, Globalization, won the first Experimental Gameplay Project competition, and is based on the theme 'consume'.
